Disney Princesses Influence Thousands of Baby Names

By Sohani Goonetillake

Disney movies have skyrocketed the popularity of the following baby names: Tiana, Elsa, Raya, Mirabel and Moana. After examining the Social Security Administration's data on baby names, we found that after the release of a Disney movie, the popularity of the protagonist's name grew exponentially.

Here are some of the most common Disney princesses people have named their children after:

Tiana is the most popular by far! However, it is important to note that Moana and Mirabel are likely lower because their name belongs to an ethnic minority.

Then the second most popular: Elsa from "Frozen".

Followed by Raya from "Raya and the Last Dragon".

Mirabel from "Encanto" was the most recent spike.

And finally, Moana has a considerably lower popularity. But considering the amount of babies named 'Moana' averaged close to zero before the movie came out, that's a pretty considerable increase.
